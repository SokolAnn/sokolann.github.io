Dissertation · AI for Social Good

Large Language Models for Scientific Discovery

Conservation, truth‑commission archives, and reliable evaluation.

I study, build, and evaluate practical tools with Prof. Nitesh Chawla. I help wildlife scientists find evidence, guide readers through Colombia’s Truth Commission archives, and make model evaluation match real work.

Care first. Then models.

Dissertation

My dissertation studies how to understand and deploy large language models in real scientific settings. We integrate LLMs with knowledge graphs, examine how people use them in practice, and test how knowledge graphs can improve reasoning, retrieval, and usefulness for applied research.
